# Getting Started with Salsa as a Service v2.0

<VersionBanner version="v2" status="current" />

Welcome to **v2.0**. This guide walks you through account setup, AI feature configuration, IoT integration, and advanced system connections.

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A valid business email address
- Access to your restaurant systems (POS, inventory, ERP)
- Admin permissions for integrations
- Optional IoT sensor hardware (for real-time quality monitoring)

<Callout type="info">
v2.0 includes an AI-powered setup wizard that recommends configuration based on your restaurant profile.
</Callout>

# Quick Setup Wizard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">

  <Step title="Configure your restaurant profile">

  <Step title="Enable AI features">

  </Step>
</Steps>

# Account Setup

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit the [Signup Page](https://app.salsaasaservice.com/signup)
2. Choose authentication method:
   - Email + 2FA
   - SSO (Google, Microsoft, Okta)
   - Enterprise SAML
3. Verify identity
4. Complete business verification

<Callout type="tip">
Enterprise customers can enable Single Sign-On during signup for seamless team access.
</Callout>

---

## Step 2: AI-Assisted Profile Configuration

Navigate to **Settings → Restaurant Profile**

### Kitchen & Infrastructure
- Production capacity
- Equipment inventory
- IoT sensor placement
- Refrigeration specs

### Operations
- Historical production volume
- Salsa varieties
- Peak times
- Waste tracking goals

### Team & Permissions
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Custom permission policies
- Biometric access (enterprise)
- Activity-based authentication rules

# AI Feature Configuration

## Demand Forecasting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Forecasting">
    Go to **AI Features → Demand Forecasting** and toggle on.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Data Sources">
    Link POS, reservations, and optional weather APIs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Import Historical Data">
    Upload at least 90 days of sales data.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Forecast Horizon">
    Choose 7, 14, or 30 days with alert thresholds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Accuracy improves over time. With 90 days of data, models reach ~70–80% accuracy and improve toward 95%+ with continued learning.
</Callout>
